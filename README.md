# RPG Statistics Screen for Minecraft Modpack
This Mod is for my modpack, trying to implement Roles and Bonuses etc.
Is supposed to enhance Minecraft in a way where you can experience different Playthroughs instead of the same old Minecraft.

# Roles
## Knight
Increased Damage, Resistance and Access to lots of new Weapons. Slight Health Increase and Slight Dexterity Increase
## Mage
Slightly decreased Health, Strength, Dexterity and Resistance, Magic Level 10 (Can use Magic Instantly)
## Assasin
Increased Strength and Dexterity, Slight Ranged Buff. All Other Stats (Health, Resistance and Magic) decreased.
## Archer
Ranged and Dexterity buff, rest of the Stats decreased slightly no Magic
## Tank
Heavily Increased Health, Resistance and Strength, Dexterity decreased and rest of the stats aswell, looses Hunger quicker.
## Larry
Starts with HEAVILY decreased Stats in ALL ranges. (Might still be worth it :P)
## Joker
Randomized Stats, could start with the worst or best character

# Stats
## Health
Increases max Health, at certain level can increase Regeneration
## Strength
Increases Melee Attack damage
## Resistance
Decreases Damage from any kind of Attack
## Dexterity
Speed, Autostep, Can climb up to 2 blocks at high level.
## Magic
Can use spells based on Magic level. Also Mana Increase.
## Ranged
More damage with Ranged weapons, Bow Abilities, and Critical hit Chance which goes through Armor
## Hunger
Higher the Stat the slower Hunger Increases
## Adaptabilty
Wont be affected by different Enviourments. Heat/Cold Affects you less.
## Special Stat: Luck (Completely Random cannot be leveled.)
Affects Loot from Chests and other things in the future
